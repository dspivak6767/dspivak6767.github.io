5G & Beyond: How Networks Are Changing Internet Speed & Access

The article “5G and Beyond: How 6G Networks Will Shape the Future of Connectivity” explains how new network technologies like 5G — and the future 6G — are making the internet faster, more reliable, and able to connect way more devices at the same time. This means less lag when streaming or gaming, better quality for video calls, and more people in remote areas getting access to the internet. These upgrades will make everyday online activities feel smoother and open the door for new innovations in business, education, and entertainment.
