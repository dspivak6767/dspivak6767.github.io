How the Internet Works

5G & Beyond: How Networks Are Changing Internet Speed & Access

The article “5G and Beyond: How 6G Networks Will Shape the Future of Connectivity” explains how newer network technologies like 5G (and the research toward 6G) will push speeds much higher, lower latency so apps feel instant, and allow many more devices to connect reliably. This means less lag when streaming or gaming, better quality for video calls, and increased access to the internet for more people in remote areas. These upgrades will make everyday online activities feel smoother and open the door for new innovations in business, education, and entertainment.
