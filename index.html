5G & Beyond: How Networks Are Changing Internet Speed & Access

The article “5G and Beyond: How 6G Networks Will Shape the Future of Connectivity” explains how newer network technologies like 5G (and the research toward 6G) will push speeds much higher, lower latency so apps feel instant, and allow many more devices to connect reliably. It's useful because faster, low-latency networks enable better remote collaboration, smoother streaming and VR, and wider internet access for rural or underserved areas — which changes how businesses and education operate.
